Currently, we are contemplating 2 color schemes: green and orange/salmon.
The green stands for fresh, healthy food.
The orange signifies warmer colors, for a more fun and homely experience.

We prioritize a more welcoming, fun color to make the experience less intimidating for inexperienced cookers. Therefore, we chose the warm salmon scheme over the green, which could feel more judgmental (signaling that you need to eat healthy and be mindful of food choices).
