A serverless data platform best known for its managed [[Redis]], designed specifically for serverless and edge environments. Upstash Redis provides instant provisioning, global replication, built-in REST APIs, and usage-based pricing, making it easy to integrate Redis without the overhead of managing infrastructure. Teams pick Upstash for its cost efficiency, simplicity, and ability to deliver low-latency caching, queues, and session storage that scale seamlessly with modern applications.

Integrates with [[Vercel]]
