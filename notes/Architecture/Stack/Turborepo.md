A high-performance build system for JavaScript and TypeScript monorepos. It speeds up development by caching, parallelizing tasks, and sharing build artifacts across environments. Teams choose Turborepo when working on multi-package or large-scale projects because it improves CI/CD performance, enforces consistency, and helps manage complex codebases without slowing developers down. It is also supported natively in [[Vercel]] deployments.
