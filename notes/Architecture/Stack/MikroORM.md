A versatile ORM supporting multiple databases ([[PostgreSQL]], MySQL, SQLite, MongoDB) with an entity-based, data-mapper pattern. It offers identity maps, caching, unit-of-work, and a rich query builder, making it feel similar to Hibernate or Doctrine. Teams choose MikroORM when building complex domain models that benefit from entity relationships, lifecycle hooks, and advanced ORM patterns.
