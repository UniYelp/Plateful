A powerful, open-source relational database known for its robustness, SQL compliance, and advanced features like JSON support, window functions, and indexing. It’s often chosen for applications that need reliability, scalability, and complex queries while still being free and community-driven. Developers pick PostgreSQL when they want a mature database with strong performance, flexible data modeling, and enterprise-level capabilities without licensing costs.
