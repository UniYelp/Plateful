A functional, concurrent programming language built on the Erlang VM (BEAM), known for its scalability and fault-tolerance. It’s especially popular for real-time, distributed systems like chat apps and IoT backends. Developers pick Elixir for its elegant syntax, massive concurrency support, and battle-tested reliability, making it ideal for long-running, high-traffic applications where uptime is critical.
