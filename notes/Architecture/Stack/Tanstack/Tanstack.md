A suite of modern, type-safe libraries for building performant and maintainable web applications in [[React]] and beyond. It includes tools like [[TanStack Query]] for data fetching, [[TanStack Router]] for navigation, [[TanStack Form]] for form validation, and [[TanStack Table]] for complex data grids—all sharing a consistent philosophy of declarative APIs, fine-grained control, and [[TypeScript]]-first design. Teams choose the TanStack ecosystem for its strong developer experience, composability, and production-ready utilities that cover most core client-side challenges with minimal overhead.
