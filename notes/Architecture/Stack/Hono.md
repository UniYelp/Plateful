A fast, lightweight web framework for building APIs, apps, and edge functions with TypeScript. It runs seamlessly on platforms like [[Vercel]], [[Convex]], and Node.js, with a minimal footprint and modern middleware system. Developers pick Hono for its speed, simplicity, and first-class [[Architecture/Stack/TypeScript]] support, making it ideal for serverless and edge-native applications where performance and developer experience are top priorities.
