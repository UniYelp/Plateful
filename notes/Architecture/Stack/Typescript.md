A superset of [[JavaScript]] that adds static typing and modern language features. It helps catch errors at compile time, improves code readability, and makes large projects more maintainable. Teams adopt TypeScript to reduce bugs, enable better tooling and autocomplete, and ensure scalability in growing codebases without sacrificing JavaScript’s flexibility.
