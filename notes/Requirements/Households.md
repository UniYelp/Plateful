A **Household** is a logical unit representing a home. it could contain multiple [[Users]], and a user may have multiple households. The users in a household all share [[Ingredients]] and [[Recipes]].

1. users should be able to view, add and remove people from their household.
2. if a user is not a part of a household, they are prompted whether to create one.
3. the people in the household are either managers (edit household members) or regular members
4. addition of users to a household is made via mail invite
